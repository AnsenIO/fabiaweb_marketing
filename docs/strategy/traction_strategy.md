## 2. Audience personas

### Persona 1: “Idea-first founder” (priority)

- **Profile:** Non-technical, 25–45, employed or freelancing, has an idea and documents (notes, PDFs, Notion).
- **Pain:** Cannot code, agencies are expensive/slow, no co-founder.
- **Desired outcome:** A launched product they can show users and investors.
- **First product:** Agentic Build Plan — €49.99/mo or €499/yr.
- **Objection:** “Can an AI really build my whole company?”
- **Content angle:** Before/after founder stories, “idea to live URL in X days.”

### Persona 2: “Operator who wants leverage”

- **Profile:** Solo consultant, creator, agency owner with existing clients.
- **Pain:** Time-constrained; wants to productise services without hiring.
- **Desired outcome:** Automated delivery + marketing for their offer.
- **First product:** Agentic Operate Plan — €49.99/mo or €499/yr.
- **Objection:** “I already use tools; why is this different?”
- **Content angle:** ROI calculators, side-by-side workflow automation.

### Persona 3: “Technical builder who wants hardware”

- **Profile:** AI engineer, researcher, CTO of small team.
- **Pain:** Cloud is expensive/locked-in; wants local high-performance AI hardware.
- **Desired outcome:** Sovereign AI workstation with support.
- **First product:** FABIABox Entry/Edge/Pro reservation (€500).
- **Objection:** “Why not just buy a DGX or build my own?”
- **Content angle:** Benchmarks, total-cost-of-ownership, support layer.

### Persona 4: “Small agency / white-label buyer”

- **Profile:** 5–20 person agency serving SMBs.
- **Pain:** Wants to offer AI agents to clients without building an AI team.
- **Desired outcome:** White-label agentic builds and operations.
- **First product:** Agentic Operate Plan → premium/white-label tier.
- **Objection:** “Is the margin worth the switching cost?”
- **Content angle:** Case studies, margin expansion, client-retention data.

## 4. Channel strategy

### Twitter/X (P0)

**Role:** highest-velocity awareness and founder narrative.

**Tactics:**
- 1 thread/week: founder transformation or “how FABIABox shipped X.”
- 3–5 daily tweets: insights, polls, quick wins, traction receipts.
- Reply thoughtfully to AI/founder/VC accounts to earn reach.
- Repurpose each thread into a LinkedIn post and short video script.

**Hashtag mix:** #SovereignAI #AICoFounder #BuildInPublic #AI #Startup #Founder #FABIABox

### LinkedIn (P0)

**Role:** B2B credibility, agency/enterprise reach, future investor channel.

**Tactics:**
- 2 posts/week: thought leadership + product updates.
- 1 article/month: deep dive on agentic company creation.
- Comment on posts by VCs, agency owners, CTOs.
- Use founder profile (Andrea / IABAI) as the primary voice.

### Short-form video — TikTok / YouTube Shorts / Reels (P1)

**Role:** explain FABIABox to non-technical founders in 30–60 seconds.

**Formats:**
- “I gave an AI my business idea and this happened…”
- “POV: your AI co-founder just shipped your company.”
- Quick myth-busters: “You don’t need to code to start a tech company.”
- Behind-the-scenes: how the FABIABox pipeline works.

**Target:** 2 videos/week, cross-posted.

### Email (P0)

**Role:** convert interest into shop actions.

**Lists:**
- Waitlist (general)
- Agentic Build interest
- Agentic Operate interest
- Hardware reservation interest
- Customers (onboarding + upsell)

**Sequences:**
- Welcome (5 emails over 14 days)
- Build Plan nurture (4 emails)
- Operate Plan nurture (4 emails)
- Hardware nurture (4 emails)
- Re-engagement (3 emails after 30 days inactive)

### SEO / blog / research page (P1)

**Role:** long-term organic demand capture.

**Pillar pages:**
- “AI Co-Founder: What It Means & How It Works”
- “From Idea to Launched Product: The FABIABox Process”
- “Sovereign AI Hardware for Startups”
- Case studies (one per quarter)

### Discord / community (P1)

**Role:** retention, feedback, social proof.

**Tactics:**
- Public server for builders and customers.
- Weekly AMA/office hours.
- Showcase shipped projects.

### Paid ads (P2)

**Rule:** only amplify organic creative that already converts.

**First tests:**
- LinkedIn sponsored content to agency owners/CTOs.
- X promoted tweets to startup/founder audiences.
- Retargeting shop visitors with specific product messaging.

## 7. Launch campaigns

### Campaign A: “Ship your company in 30 days”

- **Audience:** idea-first founders
- **Hook:** submit idea + docs → see a live product in 30 days
- **CTA:** subscribe to Agentic Build Plan
- **Channel:** X thread, LinkedIn post, short video, email
- **Proof:** documented build log (with customer permission)

### Campaign B: “€500 reservation — own your AI workstation”

- **Audience:** technical builders
- **Hook:** reserve a FABIABox before public pricing
- **CTA:** €500 reservation on shop.fabiabox.com
- **Channel:** LinkedIn, X, YouTube, technical blog
- **Proof:** specs, benchmarks, reservation counter

### Campaign C: “Agency AI layer”

- **Audience:** small agencies
- **Hook:** white-label agentic services for your clients
- **CTA:** book a discovery call / apply for pilot
- **Channel:** LinkedIn, email, partner webinars
- **Proof:** margin calculator, pilot case study
