# FABIABox Brand Voice

## Personality

- Direct, pragmatic, European
- Founder-to-founder tone
- Technical when needed, but never gatekept by jargon
- Proudly building in public

## Tagline

> The AI Co-Founder That Ships Your Company.

## Messaging rules

1. Lead with the outcome (shipped product, live company, owned stack).
2. Use EUR for all financials.
3. Avoid hype words like "revolutionary"; use proof instead.
4. Non-technical founders come first; explain acronyms.
5. Every post needs a clear CTA or conversation hook.

## Meta ad variants

Generate 4 variants per brief, covering: core message, founder transformation, traction/time-to-market, and risk reversal/no-code. Each variant needs a headline, primary text, and a Meta-approved CTA button.

## Approved CTAs

- "Start building →"
- "Pre-order for €500 →"
- "Join the waitlist →"
- "Reply with your idea →"
- "See the demo →"
